BALLICTIC LEVEL

B1-B2

Although able to withstand a 9mm, these two classes are rarely employed within Armoured vehicle's

B3-B4

These classes can withstand an assault from weapons including sporting rifles and handgun-generally, guns that are shot at close distance and lower bullet velocity such as a.357 Magnum(B3) or .44 Magnum(B4)

B5

This rating provides protection from Ak-47s and other weapons and requires a ballistic steel thinkness of aleast 7.5mm.

B6

This rating has a high level of security ans is meant to stop Armour-piercing rounds. This is still among the most popular protection levels as it is still straightforward to implement while adding a huge security advantage.

B7

The strongest protection often offered, the B7 protection level stops various armour-piercing rounds fired from super high-velocity rifles, even sniper rifles. This is many cases the highest-feasible protection availble withion civivilian-grade Armoured vehicle's Although, special purpose vehicle's can and frequently do accommodate higher protection levels.
